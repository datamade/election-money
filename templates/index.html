Data quality

We make available all raw candidate, committee and contribution data from the Illinois State Board of Elections. However, prior to July 1999, when electronic filing was introduced, much of the data is incorrect or missing.
Keep this in mind when working with it.

Candidates

Political candidates registered in Illinois. Any person who seeks nomination for election, election to, or retention in public office, or any person who seeks election as ward or township committeeman in Cook County.
Updated once a week on Tuesdays.

Committee officers

Officers for each committee. Each political committee must have both a chairman and a treasurer. One person may hold both positions. A candidate may serve as his own chairman and treasurer. No financial transactions may take place while there is a vacancy in either office.
Updated once a week on Tuesdays.

Campaign contributions

Itemized contributions to political committees in Illinois from 1989 to present. Includes any gift, subscription, donation, dues, loans, advance, deposit of money, anything of value, purchase of fund-raising tickets, or transfer of funds knowingly received to support or oppose a candidate or proposition.
Updated daily.

Campaign expenditures

Itemized expenditures by political committees. Any payment, distribution, purchase, loan, advance, deposit, gift of money or anything of value, or any transfer of funds between political committees, made to support or oppose a candidate or proposition. The definition also includes an electioneering communication.
Updated daily (though the data is released quarterly).

Committee reports

Periodic summaries of fiscal activity for committees Includes total amounts in receipts, expenditures and investment activity over a given reporting cycle, as well as a link to a detail report where possible. These files also include A-1 Reports of individual contributions over $1000
Updated daily.

Field Description

receipts Dollar amount received by the committee during the reporting period
expenditures Dollar amount spent by the committee during the reporting period
invest_total Total investments controlled by the committee at the end of the reporting period
