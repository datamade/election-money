What's in the data?

We provide six different sets of Illinois campaign finance data from 1994 to present, broken down by year:

  • Registered candidates
  • Registered political committees
  • Committee officers
  • Itemized campaign contributions
  • Itemized campaign expenditures
  • Committee fiscal report summaries

Read more about reporting requirements and data quality.

Registered candidates

Political candidates registered in Illinois. Includes any person who seeks nomination for election, election to, or retention in public office, or any person who seeks election as ward or township committeeman in Cook County. Updated once a week on Tuesdays.

Committee officers

Officers for each committee. Each political committee must have both a chairman and a treasurer. One person may hold both positions. A candidate may serve as his own chairman and treasurer. No financial transactions may take place while there is a vacancy in either office. Updated once a week on Tuesdays.

Itemized campaign contributions

Itemized contributions to political committees in Illinois. Includes any gift, subscription, donation, dues, loan, advance, deposit of money, anything of value, purchase of fundraising ticket, or transfer of funds knowingly received to support or oppose a candidate or proposition. Read more about what gets reported. Updated daily.

Itemized campaign expenditures

Itemized expenditures by political committees in Illinois. Any payment, distribution, purchase, loan, advance, deposit, gift of money or anything of value, or any transfer of funds between political committees, made to support or oppose a candidate or proposition. The definition also includes an electioneering communication. Read more about what gets reported. Updated daily (though the data is released quarterly).

Committee fiscal report summaries

Periodic summaries of fiscal activity for committees. Includes total amounts in receipts, expenditures and investment activity over a given reporting cycle, as well as a link to a detailed report where possible. These files also include A-1 Reports of individual contributions over $1,000. Updated once a week on Tuesdays.

Field definitions - Committee Reports

Field Description
id Unique ID for the report
committee_id ID of the committee that submitted the report
period_from Starting date of the reporting period
period_to Ending date of the reporting period
date_filed Date the report was filed

funds_start Dollar amount the committee had at the beginning of the reporting period
funds_end Dollar amount the committee hasd at the end of the reporting period
receipts Dollar amount received by the committee during the reporting period
expenditures Dollar amount spent by the committee during the reporting period
